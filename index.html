Welcome

This website is the home of the Allama Imdad Kazmi Digital Collection. It conserves the intellectual, historical, and cultural legacy of works by Allama Sayyid Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi (1901-1975), preserved permanently on the Internet Archive and where applicable, assigned DOIs via Zenodo.

The collection is maintained as a non commercial legacy of the extant works of the author. The aim is to ensure accurate preservation and access to his contributions to Islamic scholarship and Urdu literature.

Researchers, publishers, and readers are welcome to get in touch about using the digitised editions, translations, annotations, or curated materials presented on this site.

Building the Collection

We welcome interest from institutions and academic staff who wish to assist in the discovery, conservation, digitisation and translations of the collection. Among the works that we aim to make more widely accessible is the author's Urdu Quran translation and commentary - 'Quran ul-Mubeen: Tafsir al-Muttaqeen', originally published in Lahore, 1961.

We invite individuals, families, institutions, or collectors who may hold original publications, manuscripts, letters, or other materials relating to the author to assist in completing the historical record and help preserve the aurhor's intellectual legacy for future scholarship. Digital scans, bibliographic details, provenance information, or notices of surviving materials are all of value, and may help identify items not yet represented in the public record.

Where contributors wish to share material, we will provide guidance on rights, permissions, and making items accessible. Contributors retain ownership of their originals unless formally transferred.

To discuss a potential contribution or to notify us of surviving material, please contact us. All enquiries are acknowledged, and collaboration is welcomed.
