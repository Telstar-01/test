Welcome

This collection conserves the intellectual legacy of works by Allama Sayyid Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi (1901-1975), maintained permanently on the Internet Archive and where applicable, assigned DOIs via Zenodo. It is maintained as a non-commercial repository, ensuring preservation and access to his contributions to Islamic scholarship and Urdu literature.

Researchers, publishers, and readers are invited to get in touch about using the digitised editions, translations, annotations, or curated materials presented on this site.

Building the Collection

Interest is welcomed from institutions and academic staff who wish to assist in the discovery, conservation, digitisation and translations of the collection. It is intended to make works more widely accessible, particularly the author's Urdu Quran translation and commentary - 'Quran ul-Mubeen: Tafsir al-Muttaqeen', originally published in Lahore, 1961.

Anyone who may hold original materials relating to the author, is invited to assist in completing the historical record and help preserve the author's intellectual legacy for future scholarship. Digital scans, bibliographic details, provenance information, or notices of surviving materials are all of value, and may help identify items not yet represented in the public record.

Where contributors wish to share material, guidance on rights, permissions, and making items accessible can be provided. Contributors retain ownership of their originals unless formally transferred.
