Estate of Allama Sayyed Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi (d.1395/1975)

The literary, scholarly, and manuscript works of Allama Sayyed Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi (d. 1975, Pakistan) remain protected under the Copyright Ordinance of Pakistan (1962), which provides copyright protection for the lifetime of the author plus fifty (50) years. Accordingly, these works remain under copyright until 2025.

Upon the author’s passing, all associated copyright, moral rights, and literary rights became part of his estate, to be inherited in accordance with Pakistani succession law.

As lawful descendants and heirs of the author, the undersigned family members constitute the present holders and custodians of these rights.

The family asserts the following:

  1. Ownership: The copyright in all published and unpublished works of Allama Sayyed Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi is held by the Estate of Allama Sayyed Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi, represented by his surviving descendants.
  2. Scope of Rights: These rights include, but are not limited to: – the right to reproduce the works – the right to publish or republish the works – the right to digitise and preserve the works – the right to authorise or refuse derivative works – the right to control distribution, public display, and online availability – the right to maintain the integrity and attribution of the works (moral rights)
  3. Purpose of Custodianship: The Estate manages these works solely as a non commercial cultural and scholarly legacy, with the aim of preserving the author’s intellectual contributions and ensuring responsible access for future generations.
  4. Permissions: Any individual, institution, publisher, or researcher seeking to reproduce, publish, translate, or otherwise use the works must obtain written permission from the Estate.
  5. Contact for Rights & Permissions: All correspondence regarding rights, permissions, or scholarly use should be directed to: Estate of Allama Sayyed Imdad Hussain Kazmi al-Mashhadi [Contact email]
