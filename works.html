Journal Publishing

Allama Imdad Kazmi was Chief Supervisor of the journal - Ma'arif-e-Islam, edited by Shaykh Ghiyasuddin and published monthly in Lahore by the institution bearing the same name. It contained a range of scholarly articles and news from the Indo-Pak Shia community, including a dedicated section for women and notices of new publications. Allama Imdad also served as President of Idara Ma'arif-e-Islam for four years. Below are samples from the 1950s through to the 1970s, including those held at the digital library of the Center for the Restoration of Antiquities of the Subcontinent (MAAB). The 1963 edition mentions the availability of Allama Imdad Kazmi's own published works, Tafsir al-Muttaqeen (1961) and Akhlaq-ul-Ma'sumeen (1960).
