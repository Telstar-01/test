Journal Publishing

Allama Imdad was Chief Supervisor of the journal - Ma'arif-e-Islam - published monthly in Lahore by the institution bearing the same name. It contained a range of articles and news, including a dedicated section for women and notices of new publications. Allama Imdad also served as President of Idara Ma'arif-e-Islam for four years. Below are samples from the 1950s through to the 1970s. The 1963 edition mentions the availability of Allama Imdad Kazmi's own published works, Tafsir al-Muttaqeen (1961) and Akhlaq-ul-Ma'sumeen (1960).
